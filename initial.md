# Sharlyte Staffing Solutions — Website Design & Structure Instructions

## Project Overview

We are building a premium healthcare staffing agency website for Sharlyte Staffing Solutions using:

- Next.js
- TailwindCSS
- Antigravity

This website is NOT a SaaS platform or tech startup website.

The visual identity should feel like:

- Premium healthcare staffing agency
- Luxury consulting firm
- Human-centered healthcare recruitment brand
- Warm, trustworthy, and professional

The website should emotionally communicate:

- Trust
- Care
- Professionalism
- Opportunity
- Guidance
- Long-term partnerships

Avoid making the design feel:
- futuristic
- overly technical
- startup-like
- dashboard-heavy
- AI-generated
- cyber/tech themed

---

# Important Design Direction

We are following the CONTENT STRUCTURE and PAGE DISTRIBUTION from:

https://orificehealth.com/

IMPORTANT:
- DO NOT copy their colors
- DO NOT copy their visual branding
- DO NOT copy exact layouts
- DO NOT copy their styling

We only want:
- similar page flow
- similar section distribution
- similar content hierarchy
- similar structural organization

Use dummy content and placeholder sections initially.

The goal is:
- build the structural skeleton first
- later we will redesign and customize each section manually

- minimal
